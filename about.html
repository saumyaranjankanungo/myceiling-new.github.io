Who we are

MyCeiling Private Limited is the leader and innovator in lighting, ceiling and wall in India. Our manufacturing unit and corporate office are based out in Bangalore (Karnataka) serving PAN India.

It was founded by Jay Sah in 2016 after quitting his job at Livspace.com. Years of R&D with patents filing and the journey begins. For the right brand positioning MyCeiling collaborated with leading home décor brands like Livspace, Homelane, Pepperfry, Mygubbi, Designcafe & more and its products been showcased in the complementing ambiance.

After expanding its productline MyCeiling started establishing own dealers and distributors to avail physical presence at various cities to the end users. Post lockdown there have been an unexpected rise in the number of people who are willing to start own business and MyCeiling innovative products became preference due to low competition.

If you believe your place should tell the story of who you’re, we’ll be the perfect place to shop at.

Vision


How about having fan, false ceiling, music system, home automation kits all in one product? Sounds magical right. That’s why we call it tech driven Smart Ceiling. MyCeiling is the future of home décor.

With “Make in India” spirit in our heart we aim towards making a leading Indian company in home & corporate décor market to offer more functional and innovative home electronic products. People are switching to online shopping losing their patience to wait for long at vendors, concerned about branded quality product and a hassle free experience. They want everything at a place rather than purchasing multiple products from multiple vendors. Reading this behaviour of customers we add another innovative feature to our product launch restricting the competition with IPR.

Eco-System In Home Décor


We believe in maintaining the balance in the eco-system of a market, not in disrupting it. The existing market has manufacturers, franchise owners, distributers, retailers and customers. If we hold the power of innovation we also remember our responsibility to use it wisely. We designed our business model in such a way that everyone in the existing market gets business when we offer our innovative products. We look forward to have 500+ MyCeiling dealers & distributors across India and offer both medium and premium price products to make it affordable to every homeowner (whether existing home or new one) in next 2 years.
